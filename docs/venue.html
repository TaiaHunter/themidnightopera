The Midnight Opera

The midnight opera is a VRChat world commissioned by Taia_Hunter, and then made by Rinvo.

The world is optimized and quest compatible.

There are all sorts of props, backdrops and particles to mix and match into all sorts of stage sets.

At the end of the day, we are a theatre and hope to bring magic and imagination that a real theatre can into the VR Space. You can visit here!

Taia Hunter Productions

Taia Hunter Productions is a world in progress. Commissioned by Taia_Hunter, and currently being built by ZarcxD.

THP is meant to be for the Wednesday bar nights only. A smaller venue as well as an art gallery, featuring Taia's work.

At the moment the world is still in the very early stages, so it is still in the works. However you can visit it here!
